# Image Generation Prompt

## Specifications

- Platform: Instagram
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Target audience: Restaurant managers and owners
- Style: Simple, clean, minimal text, AI-driven feel
- Design: Japanese-friendly aesthetic

## Prompt

1:1 square image. A sleek, modern, and informative design for an Instagram carousel slide targeting restaurant owners. The main Japanese text '毎日イチから文章を考えてるのが原因' (The cause is thinking up text from scratch every day) is prominently displayed in a large, bold, and clear Japanese font. The background visually represents inefficiency or repetitive manual work in an abstract, modern way. This could be a subtly fractured or looping pattern of a keyboard, a repetitive motion graphic of a pen, or faint, broken digital lines suggesting an unoptimized process. The overall design emphasizes the burden of starting from zero each time. Colors are professional, like grays, dark blues, and subtle, non-alarming reds to highlight the 'problem.' Clean, AI-generated aesthetic. Japanese design aesthetic.
