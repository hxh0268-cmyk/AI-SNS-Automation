# Image Generation Prompt

## Specifications

- Platform: Instagram
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Target audience: Restaurant managers and owners
- Style: Simple, clean, minimal text, AI-driven feel
- Design: Japanese-friendly aesthetic

## Prompt

1:1 square image. A clean, modern, and empathetic design for an Instagram carousel slide targeting restaurant owners. The main Japanese text '閉店後、何書くか悩んでいませんか？' (After closing, are you struggling with what to write?) is prominently displayed in a large, clear, and slightly contemplative Japanese font. The background depicts a subtle, abstract representation of a quiet, dimly lit restaurant interior after closing, perhaps with a soft glow from a digital screen. Overlaid are faint, abstract thought bubbles or question marks composed of subtle data lines, symbolizing contemplation or a struggle for ideas. The mood is reflective but offers a hint of future relief. Colors are muted blues, grays, and soft ambient light, maintaining a clean, AI-generated aesthetic. Japanese design aesthetic.
