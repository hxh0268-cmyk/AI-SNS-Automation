# Image Generation Prompt

## Specifications

- Platform: Instagram
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Target audience: Restaurant managers and owners
- Style: Simple, clean, minimal text, AI-driven feel
- Design: Japanese-friendly aesthetic

## Prompt

1:1 square image. A vibrant, modern, and success-oriented design for an Instagram carousel slide targeting restaurant owners. The main Japanese text '下書きをAIに任せたら10分で完成！' (Delegating drafts to AI completed in 10 minutes!) is prominently displayed in a large, bold, and empowering Japanese font. The background vividly illustrates efficiency and AI assistance: fast-moving data streams, a stylized, glowing AI brain or core processing unit, and a digital clock rapidly counting down, indicating quick completion. The overall impression is one of speed, automation, and effortless accomplishment through AI. Colors are bright and optimistic, such as vibrant greens, energetic blues, and clean whites, all contributing to a high-tech, AI-generated feel. Japanese design aesthetic.
